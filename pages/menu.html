Burger

A burger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked ground meat or vegetable patty, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun. They are a popular and versatile food found globally, from fast food to gourmet restaurants. Common additions include cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and various sauces like ketchup or mustard, and many variations exist, such as cheeseburgers and bacon burgers.

Chocolate cake

Chocolate cake .is a popular, sweet, and versatile dessert flavored with melted chocolate or cocoa powder, often served during celebrations and holidays. Its history dates back to the 19th century, evolving from a chocolate beverage to the baked good we know today thanks to advancements in cocoa processing. Available in many variations, chocolate cake can be made home or purchased, enjoyed by people of all ages, and is frequently paired with various icings, frostings, and fillings.
